How To Use

Four steps. Never skip step one.

1

Test on a common card first

Always try it on a card you don't care about before touching anything valuable.

2

Spray the cloth — never the card

One or two mists onto a clean microfiber cloth. Direct spray can wick into edges.

3

One light pass

Wipe gently in one direction across the card face. Don't scrub.

4

Buff dry immediately

Flip to the dry side of the cloth and buff. Check under angled light before sleeving.

Collector Safety

Know your card before you clean it

Safe to clean

  • Modern glossy Pokemon & TCG cards
  • Modern coated sports cards & Chrome
  • Holos and reverse holos (light pass)

Keep it away from

  • Autographed or hand-numbered cards — alcohol lifts ink
  • Vintage / pre-1990s or raw-paper stock — moisture wicks & warps
  • Card edges and corners — wipe the face only
This is a surface cleaner — it removes dirt and fingerprints. It does not alter, add to, or "fix" the card itself, and it's designed to leave nothing behind that a grader could ever flag.
